# Privacy Policy

# TaskHeap**

**Effective Date: 2025-03-30**

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how TaskHeap ("we," "us," or "our") handles user
data.

## 2. Data Collection

TaskHeap can be used entirely without an account.
If you choose to create an account, we collect the following information:

- **Email address** — used for authentication
- **Display name** — used to identify your account

No other personal information is collected.
We do not collect usage analytics, device identifiers, IP addresses for tracking purposes, or any other data beyond what is listed above.

## 3. Optional Sync and Account Features

Creating an account enables data synchronization across your devices.
All synced data is end-to-end encrypted —
we cannot read the contents of your data.
The only metadata visible to our servers is the submission timestamp of each
data update.
If you do not create an account,
all your data remains exclusively on your device.

## 4. How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used solely to:

- Authenticate you and provide access to your account
- Sync your data across devices

We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising, analytics,
or any purpose beyond operating the service.

## 5. Data Retention and Deletion

You may delete your account at any time by contacting us at the address below.
Upon deletion, your account information (email and name) and all associated
data will be permanently removed from our servers.

## 6. Data Security

All synced data is encrypted, and only submission timestamps are accessible
in unencrypted form on our servers.
Account credentials are stored using industry-standard security practices.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy on
this page.
The effective date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last
revised.
We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

## 8. Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, or to request account deletion, please contact us at:
taskheap+privacy@pm.me

## 9. Consent

By using TaskHeap, you consent to this Privacy Policy.
